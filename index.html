Exercise Technical Reference

Proper structural biomechanics to maximize workout efficiency.

1. Incline Push-Up

Place hands on an elevated surface (couch/bed/sturdy table). Align spine without letting your hips sag. Lower chest fully and push back up dynamic and fast.

2. Bodyweight Squat

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Sit backwards as if landing in a low chair. Keep chest up, press back through your heels, and lock out glutes.

3. Forearm Plank

Balance entirely on elbows and toes. Squeeze abdomen, thighs, and glutes to keep the body completely parallel to the ground.

4. Paced Spin Bike Setup

Keep resistance low/medium. Maintain a comfortable breathing pace where you are sweating but not gasping. Perfect steady cardio stimulus.

5. Weighted Water-Bottle Jog Hack

Hold 1L/1.5L water bottles in each hand while jogged at speed 8-9. Increases work rate of shoulders and core, safely scaling intensity.
